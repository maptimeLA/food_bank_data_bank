EBT_Restaurants_list.txt lists restaurants participating in the CalFresh Restaurant Meals Program.

The source data, along with other info, is provided by the Los Angeles County Department of Public Social Services at http://www.ladpss.org/dpss/restaurant_meals/.

The semicolon-delimited text file was extracted from August 15 pdf:
http://www.ladpss.org/apps/files/download.cfm?file=2402&folder=249
